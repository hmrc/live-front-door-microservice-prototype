What is the business priority level?

To ensure the highest priority requests are assessed accurately and actioned accordingly, please assign your request as accurately as possible using the following descriptions as guidance.

What does low priority mean?
Select if request resembles any of the following:
  • between 1-9 reports of users affected
  • service failures of up to 9%
  • legislative changes with non imminent deadlines, for example; a deadline of over three months would be categorised as medium priority.
What does medium priority mean?
Select if request resembles any of the following:
  • 49 or more reports of users affected
  • service failures of between 10-49%
  • legislative changes with non imminent deadlines, for example; a deadline of upto six weeks would be categorised as medium priority.
What does high priority mean?
Select if request resembles any of the following:
  • 50 or more reports of users affected
  • service failures in excess of 50%
  • legislative changes with imminent deadlines, for example; a deadline of: immediate to one month would be categorised as high priority.
